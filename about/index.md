# About Chrome Extension Guide

The Chrome Web Store has 200,000+ extensions and most of them are garbage. These guides help you find the ones worth installing — and warn you about the ones that will steal your data or slow your browser to a crawl.

Every extension review includes real performance impact measurements. When an extension requests suspicious permissions, I explain exactly what that means for your data. No paid placements, no affiliate rankings.

## Content Standards

Every article is reviewed for accuracy. Outdated information is updated or removed. No sponsored placements or paid reviews.
